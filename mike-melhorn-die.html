How Did Mike Melhorn Die? Know Everything About this Incident

Mike Melhorn lost his life in a serious two-car collision on Interstate 81 on Saturday morning. The reports indicate that Mike Melhorn passed away due to the injuries he suffered in the car accident. To get a deeper understanding of this incident, continue reading this article.

How Did Mike Melhorn Die?

Around 1:45 a.m., a tragic incident unfolded on Interstate 81 near mile marker 67.5 in Susquehanna Township. According to authorities, a car was traveling southbound on the highway when it suddenly veered off the road and onto the grassy median, which had a raised central berm. In a sequence of events, the car lost control and crossed into the left lane of I-81 North. There, it collided with a trailer being towed by another vehicle also traveling northward on the highway. The impact of the collision caused the initial car to flip over onto its roof, as confirmed by law enforcement sources.

Sadly, the collision led to the loss of Mike Melhorn’s life, with authorities pronouncing him dead as a consequence of the crash.
