Renee Paquette Gets Involved In Jon Moxley Angle On AEW Dynamite

AEW has a few couples on their roster, but Renee Paquette and Jon Moxley are very happy to be in the same company. That being said, the real-life married couple haven’t been involved in any angles yet. That changed this week on AEW Dynamite.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite this week.

This week’s episode of AEW Dynamite kicked off with Jon Moxley and Ishii having a tremendous battle. Everything came to a head when Eddie Kingston got involved.

Backstage after the match, Renee Paquette was seen confronting Jon Moxley over how things went during that opening match. Then Eddie Kingston got involved as well.

Renee Paquette had to literally step in between Eddie Kingston and Jon Moxley as they argued backstage. After Moxley left, Renee poked Eddie in the chest and ordered for him to fix things.

We will have to see how this goes, but it is the first time we’ve seen Renee Paquette in an angle on AEW television, and a backstage segment where she wasn’t conducting an interview. You can check out the segment below.
