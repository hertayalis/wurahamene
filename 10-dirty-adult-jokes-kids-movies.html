10 Dirty Adult Jokes From Childrens Movies

6) Flashers – Cars (2006)

Recommended Videos

Many saw Cars as a blemish on Pixar’s otherwise stellar filmography, but the animation studio still managed to insert the occasional moment of brilliance into their weakest link… and by brilliance, I mean moment of smut. However, in a world inhabited solely by talking cars, how do you even manage to perform such a feat?

At the beginning of Cars, Lightning McQueen is one of the most popular cars in the entire racing world and is adored by fans all over the wrold. Two groupies in particular, named Mia and Tia, decide to show their support by flashing their headlights rapidly on and off as McQueen passes. Sounds pretty cute, right?

Wait, let’s look at that again. Mia and Tia flash McQueen as he passes. Flash. If these cars were human, their nipples would be exposed out in the air, harder than diamonds.
