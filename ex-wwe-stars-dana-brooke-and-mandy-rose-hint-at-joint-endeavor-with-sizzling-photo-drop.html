Ex-WWE Stars Dana Brooke and Mandy Rose Hint at Joint Endeavor with Sizzling Photo Drop

Dana Brooke, the former WWE dynamo renowned for her remarkable athleticism and devoted fan base, bid farewell to the company just last month. Since her release, she’s been busy with her Premium Content Service is proving to be incredibly profitable for her. Now she has promised to do a collaboration with Mandy Rose in the future.

While Dana Brooke’s WWE departure took her supporters by surprise, it certainly didn’t dampen her determination. In fact, Brooke is determined to prove all of her naysayers wrong now.

Dana Brooke was asked on Instagram if they could expect her to collaborate with Mandy or Macey. Brooke made it clear that a collaboration with Rose would happen as she uploaded photos of her and Mandy Rose in bikinis.

“Isn’t that a silly question!? Duhhhh! Stay tuned!”

We will have to wait and see when Dana Brooke and Mandy Rose will drop their collaboration, as it is sure to break the internet one way or the other. After all, their brand value has shot up following their WWE release.
