Dave Mustaine Net Worth

Dave Mustaine is a legendary musician who was born on September 13, 1961 in La Mesa, CA. He is best known for his work as the former guitarist for Metallica and the singer and guitarist for the heavy metal band Megadeth. Mustaine has been credited with helping to shape the sound of thrash metal and has been inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
