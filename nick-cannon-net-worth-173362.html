Nick Cannon Net Worth

Nick Cannon is an American actor, comedian, rapper, and television host. He was born on October 8, 1980 in San Diego, CA and is best known for his roles on Nickelodeon's All That and hosting The Nick Cannon Show, Wild 'N Out, and America's Got Talent. He is also a recording artist, having released his self-titled debut album in 2003 and his song "Parents Just Don't Understand" was featured in the film Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius.
