Supermodels Pay Tribute to George Michael

Last night, it was announced that George Michael had passed away at the age of 53 on Christmas day in his North London home.

Not only was Michael a trailblazer in the music world, he also left a lasting impact on the fashion industry. From his iconic 1990 "Freedom!" music video starring supermodels Naomi Campbell, Linda Evangelista, Cindy Crawford, Christy Turlington and Tatjana Patitz to his outspoken support of gay rights, Michael created a legacy of his own within the fashion world.
