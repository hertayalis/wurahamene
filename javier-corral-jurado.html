Biography

He played a large role in the constitutional challenge that put an end to the controversial Televisa Law in 2007. He was named after actor and singer Javier Solís who had died a few months before he was born. He became a member of the National Action Party in 1982.

Career

He is a Mexican politician affiliated with the National Action Party (PAN) and the current governor of Chihuahua, who took office on 4 October 2016. He has been active in politics since the early 1980s, including six terms between the Chamber of Deputies and the Senate. Corral also specializes in communications and has a long career as a columnist and founder of various publications; he has also been instrumental in the successful constitutional challenge brought to bear by the Televisa Law.
