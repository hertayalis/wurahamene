A Stylist Told Me That These Are the Top 4 Fashion Trends for Spring

If you’re like me, your sweats got a little too much love this winter — but let’s not talk about that. Spring is officially here, the sun is finally shining, and I’m ready to reinvent my wardrobe to welcome the season in style. Naturally, I’m turning to Amazon’s impressive fashion selection, and I’m recruiting backup; Stylist Catherine Bibeau spilled her top four spring 2024 trend predictions with InStyle, and I found everything you need to embrace these looks.   

As Bibeau said, “trends are all about dressing up with freedom” and making popular pieces your own. So, allow the following five picks to serve as your fashion inspiration and kickstart your spring shopping haul. Below, browse through clothing, shoes, and accessories fit for every occasion this season, from $30. Just be sure to grab your favorite finds soon, these trendy picks are bound to sell out quickly. 

Stylist-Approved Spring Trends

High-Rise Trousers 

If you, too, are a low-rise jean hater, you’ll be thrilled to learn that high-rise waistlines are here to stay. In my book, the comfortable-yet-flattering silhouette is always in, but Bibeau confirmed we’ll be seeing the style, notably, in trousers this season. To get the look, snag the oh-so-chic Amalia trousers from Amazon’s influencer-loved brand, The Drop. The pants have an effortlessly elevated look thanks to their relaxed shape and stylish pleats. A major bonus: They have a sneaky elastic waistband for easy all-day wear. Throw the breezy pair on with your favorite sneakers and white tee, or dress it up with slingbacks (cough, cough see below) and a coordinating blazer.     

Pointed-Toe Slingbacks   

Did someone say slingbacks? The sophisticated shoes are on the feet of every it-girl this spring. To name a few: Julia Roberts, Katie Holmes, Camila Mendes, and Hailey Bieber have all been spotted sporting the footwear silhouette. Bibeau confirmed the shoes will only rise in popularity as the season continues, specifically styles with pointed-toes. If you’re in the market for a pair yourself, look no further than these Franco Sarto Racerback pumps, which happen to be 25 percent off. The shoes have a 2-inch block heel, and, of course, a sexy slingback strap you can adjust to your perfect fit. While I’m partial to the leopard print, which is also having a major renaissance this season, you can grab the pumps in 27 additional colorways.  

Classic Polos 

Another stylist-approved trend to snag at the start of the season: polo shirts. The classic tops instantly elevate any look; just swap one in place of your go-to T-shirt and watch as it completely transforms your ensemble. Bibeau recommends pairing a polo with a “ruffled micro-mini skirt” to add a hyper-feminine flair to the unisex style. This Imily Bela option is marked down to $33 from its usual $47 price tag, so you’ll want to grab it ASAP. The polo iteration has a lightweight cable knit material, making it the perfect transitional wardrobe pick. Bibeau added that monochromatic whites will also be huge this season, and “playing with different fabric and texture,” as this top does, is the best way to create a unique yet on-trend look.     

Metallic Accessories   

Since no shopping spree is complete without at least one new accessory, you’ll want to snag The Drop’s silver Southampton clutch for just $30. Bibeau predicts that metallics, particularly silver, will dominate the accessory space this spring. The bag is sure to make a statement with any outfit, though it’s not too flashy thanks to its subdued pebbled texture. Not only is the versatile clutch fashion-forward, but it’s functional, too; it’s the perfect size to stow your essentials. Style it with jeans and a tee, or tote it to any special events on the calendar this spring. Still on the fence? Take it from one shopper who said they were “blown away” by the quality of the bag since it’s “buttery soft and looks amazing.”   

Run to Amazon to snag the stylist-approved spring trends that catch your eye before they sell out.
