Here's How Much Money Donnie Yen Is Actually Worth

Ethan Miller/Getty Images

Donnie Yen's name might not quite make the list of martial arts actors everyone automatically knows about, like Bruce Lee, Jackie Chan and Jet Li. However, that's simply because the world hasn't been paying attention. The martial arts expert's IMDb page is a showcase of a career spent well, from his lead role in the Ip Man franchise to a number of high-profile Hollywood roles, from his stint as Chirrut Imwe in Rogue One: A Star Wars Story to his announced role as the Emperor in Disney's live-action version of Mulan. What's more, he's a particularly brave Kung Fu actor because he's not afraid to leave his significant body of martial arts work behind him: In 2019, the 56-year-old Yen told South China Morning Post that Ip Man 4 will be his last Kung Fu film: "As an actor, you must keep going forward," Yen says. "This is something that I must do in life; I must keep exploring to grow." 

It's a pretty bold move for a man predominantly known for his martial arts movies, though it's easy to assume Yen realizes he may be getting a bit old for all those fancy moves. It almost makes you think that he must have a decent enough nest egg on his bank account so he doesn't have to worry about going broke even if his future cinematic aspirations won't go as planned. The question is, then: Just how much money is Donnie Yen worth?
