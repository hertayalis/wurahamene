Ellen Hollman Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Ellen Hollman was born on April 1, 1983 in Detroit, Michigan, USA. She started acting on television in the mid 2000s, first appearing on such shows as Sex, Love & Secrets and Malcolm in the Middle. In 2006, she made her film debut in the movie Surf School, followed by Road House 2. She next appeared as Ivy in the horror movie Asylum released in 2007. The following year, she starred as Alison in the indie movie Fling.

In the late 2000s, she landed one-episode roles in Criminal Minds, The O.C., Weeds, and In Plain Sight, to name a few. She then became known for her role as Saxa in Spartacus: Vengeance in 2012 and Spartacus: War of the Damned in 2013. Recently, she played the role of Valina in the direct-to-video feature The Scorpion King 4: Quest for Power. She has also made guest appearances in CSI: Crime Scene Investigation and NCIS.
